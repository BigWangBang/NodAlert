1. Information Collection

When you use NodAlert:

  • Camera Data: We use your device's front-facing camera to analyze facial features in real-time to detect your concentration state. Camera images are processed locally on your device only and will not be uploaded to our servers or any third parties.

All data we collect is used solely for the following purposes:
